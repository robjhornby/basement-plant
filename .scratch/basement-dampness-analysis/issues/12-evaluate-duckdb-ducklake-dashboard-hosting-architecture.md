## Question

What simple, cheap architecture should ingest daily CSV emails into DuckDB or DuckLake and publish a dashboard on `robjhornby.com`?

Research options that fit the user's learning goal around DuckDB/DuckLake while staying operationally simple. Compare storage format, scheduler/automation, dashboard framework, static versus dynamic hosting, deployment target, secrets handling, and backup strategy. Identify what would be overkill but educational versus what is necessary.
